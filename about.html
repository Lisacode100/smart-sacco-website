About Us

Who We Are

Our Purpose

At SmartSacco, we believe no one should be excluded from financial security, especially the visually impaired. Born out of Makerere University's innovation hub, we have the vision to transform SACCOs with accessible technology.

Our Why

We watched visually impaired members struggle with paper ledgers, reliance on others, and fraud. Today, they use voice commands to deposit via MTN Mobile Money, check balances, and more, all without sight.

Our Journey

Starting as a university project at Makerere University, we've grown into a comprehensive solution serving over 50 SACCOs with 10,000+ members across East Africa.
