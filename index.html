Problems We Solve

Addressing key challenges in traditional SACCO systems

Financial Insecurity

Manual record-keeping leads to errors, fraud, and lost savings.

Accessibility Barriers

Traditional SACCOs exclude visually impaired users.

Operational Inefficiency

Slow, paper-based processes delay transactions.

Lack of Trust

No real-time tracking or audit trails enable fraud.

Our Objectives

Driving financial inclusion through technology

Primary Goals

  • Make SACCO services accessible (voice navigation, screen reader support)
  • Automate processes (mobile money integration)
  • Build trust via transparent dashboards

Secondary Goals

  • Expand financial inclusion for marginalized groups
  • Scale regionally across East Africa
