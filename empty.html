Welcome to SmartSacco

A revolutionary digital platform transforming SACCO operations through automation, accessibility, and transparency.

Our Mission

To empower SACCOs with secure, accessible financial tools that foster trust and inclusion for all members, with special focus on marginalized communities in East Africa.

Challenges & Solutions

Problems We Address

  • Manual record-keeping errors and fraud
  • Inaccessibility for visually impaired users
  • Slow, paper-based processes
  • Lack of real-time transaction tracking

Our Solutions

  • Automated digital record-keeping
  • Voice navigation & screen reader support
  • Mobile money integration
  • Real-time transaction dashboards

Key Features

  • Mobile Integration: Apply for loans and make payments via mobile money
  • Accessibility: Voice UI and high-contrast mode for visual impairment
  • Real-Time Tracking: Instant updates on savings and loans
  • Security: Fraud prevention with audit trails
  • Admin Dashboard: Comprehensive member management

Project Objectives

Primary Goals

  • Automate SACCO processes
  • Enable accessibility features
  • Build trust through transparency

Future Vision

  • Expand across East Africa
  • Integrate with banking systems
  • AI-powered financial advice
